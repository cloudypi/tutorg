Cloudy Pi



What's Cloudy Pi ?

Cloudy Pi is a software that turns your Raspberry Pi in a real cloud server. Cloudy Pi is based on privacy, open-source and ease of use.

Built with PHP, MySQL and Javascript.

Upload and download your files fastely.

Don't worry about security and privacy, you're the only owner of your datas.
